\section{LLMs und Prompting}
\label{sec:llms}

Before we can delve into the specifics of prompt injection attacks and their defences, it is crucial to understand the underlying technology: Large Language Models (LLMs) and the concept of prompting. \\
LLMs are AI models that have been trained on vast amounts of text data to understand and generate human-like language. They are designed to predict the next word in a sentence, which allows them to generate coherent and contextually relevant text based on the input they receive.  \textbf{CITATION NEEDED}\\
